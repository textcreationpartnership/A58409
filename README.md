#####Body#####

1. A true Account of His Holineſs Proceſſion at Aberdene, January 11. 1689. Which was ſuppoſed to have been performed by His Holineſs in the greateſt Splendor, having come on purpoſe to Congratulat the good Succeſs the Roman-Catholicks had in this Kingdom, & more particularly in Aberdene: In order to which His Holineſs being Seated on a Chair of State with His Crown on His Head, His Keys on His Arm, and Clothed with Scarlet Robes Lined with Ermine; Began His Proceſſion from the Colledge-Gate, about four a-Clock in the Afternoon, and was accompanied as follows.
